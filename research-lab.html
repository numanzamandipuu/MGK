Research Lab

Md. Golam Kibria oversees and manages the following labs in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at RUET: Metrology Lab, Heat Engine Lab, Fluid Mechanics Lab, Machine Shop

  1. Home
  2. Research Lab

Metrology Lab

Facilitates precision measurement and quality control experiments, ensuring students gain hands-on experience with advanced metrology tools.

Heat Engine Lab

Provides practical knowledge on thermodynamic cycles, engine performance testing, and energy efficiency analysis.

Fluid Mechanics Lab

Supports experiments on fluid properties, flow dynamics, and hydraulic systems, enhancing students' understanding of theoretical concepts.

Machine Shop

Equipped with lathes, milling machines, and other tools for machining processes, where Mr. Kibria oversees safety and operational efficiency.

Welding Shop

Offers training in welding techniques, materials joining processes, and safety protocols under his guidance.

Wood Shop

Enables hands-on projects involving woodworking tools and techniques, ensuring practical skill development.
