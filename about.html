About

Md. Golam Kibria, born in Rajshahi, Bangladesh, earned his B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering from RUET in 2017, receiving the Prime Minister Gold Medal (2017) and the University Gold Medal for outstanding academic performance. He served as a Lecturer in RUET's ME department (2019-2022) and is now an Assistant Professor there since July 2022. He has notable publications in his research areas.
